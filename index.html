About Me

Hey! My name is Mike Fox, I'm from the District of Columbia area (DMV). I am currently pursuing a carreer in web development with a focus of front-end engineering with a full-stack education.


I come from a long career in the restaurant industry with a focus of hospitality and management. I was the General Manager of one of the best neighbiorhood bars in DC. I spent well over a decade working my way up, starting from a food runner all the way up to managing a 3 million dollar a year buisness and oveerseeing every aspect from the kitchen to front of house service.


I decided to make a change in my life and pursue developing. I've always had a knack for technology and learning how things work. As much as I love my carreer in hospitality I was ready to jump into something new. Plus, the idea of having weekends off is pretty inticing.


Little more about my actual life! I have two cats, and a Australian Sheppard. I've lived in DC proper for almost a decade and grew up in the area, I've seen this city transform over the last 30 years. I used ti be an avid gamer but now I just follow the industry. Big fan of true crime podcasts and docs. Aspiring "chef", whiskey snob, a guy who thinks he knows wine, and a world traveler.
